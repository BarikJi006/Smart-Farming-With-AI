Simple Setup

Up & Running
in One Day

No electrician, no engineer — our kit is designed for any farmer to set up alone, with step-by-step Hindi + English instructions.

1

Install the Sensors

Push our wireless soil sensors into the ground across your field zones. They connect automatically via Bluetooth.

2

Connect the Hub

Plug the AgroFlow hub near your pump. It talks to all sensors and controls your irrigation valves.

3

Tell Us Your Crops

Select what you're growing — wheat, rice, vegetables, sugarcane. The app loads the perfect moisture profile.

4

Watch Your Farm Thrive

AgroFlow irrigates automatically. You get live updates on your phone and your crops get exactly what they need.
